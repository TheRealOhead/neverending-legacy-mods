Owen's Neverending Legacy Mods
Any of these links can be copy/pasted or dragged into the mod menu
Cremation
During the endgame, people start dying faster than they can be buried. Cremation is the solution! It's a mode for firekeepers, unlocked from ironworking.
https://therealohead.github.io/neverending-legacy-mods/cremation.js
Custom Map
My good pal Damien was unhappy about the size of the map, so I went ahead and gave control of map size to the user.
https://therealohead.github.io/neverending-legacy-mods/big-map.js
Meditation
During the endgame, it is literally not possible to generate fast ticks without pausing the game. Meditaters can help with that.
https://therealohead.github.io/neverending-legacy-mods/meditater.js
